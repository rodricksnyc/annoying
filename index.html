Frequently Asked Questions

What is this study about?

  • The symptoms that people experience when they have cancer are an important component of their health.
  • We are conducting this study to explore patients’ experiences with different symptoms in order to understand how symptom frequency, severity and interference affect how people feel and function.

Who is conducting this survey?

  • This study is being conducted by the National Cancer Institute in collaboration with the State Cancer Registries.
  • The study was approved by the Institutional Review Board of the National Institutes of Health.

How is the information collected?

  • The information is collected using an online survey.
  • Paper surveys will be used by participants who do not have internet access or who prefer to use a paper survey.

How was I selected for this study?

  • You were invited to participate in this study through your state cancer registry.
  • Each state collects information from hospitals and maintains a state-wide cancer registry.
  • More information about the cancer registries program is available from the Center for Disease Control.

How long will the study take?

  • Most people will be able to complete the study survey in about 15 minutes. You may need more or less time.
  • This study consists of a survey only. Your participation will be complete after you answer the survey items.

How will my privacy be protected?

  • All the information you give to this study will be kept private, and your answers will not be connected with your name or any other personal information.
  • Only group level or summary results will be shared or published.
  • You will be offered an Amazon Gift Card and you may elect to have the gift card number e-mailed to you. If you provide your e-mail address for this purpose, we will not retain it after sending you your gift card number.
